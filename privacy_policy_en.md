### 2. Our Privacy Principles

- **Data minimization** — we only collect what is necessary for the App to function
- **Transparency** — we clearly describe what is collected and why
- **Local-first** — your personal data (diary, ratings, notes) is stored exclusively on your device
- **Security** — all data transmission uses encrypted channels (HTTPS only)

### 3. What Data We Collect and Why

#### 3.1 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not request or store on our servers:
- Name, email address, phone number
- Diary content: entries, notes, mood tags, episode and drama ratings
- Favorites lists or watch statuses
- Location data
- Photos or media files

All of the above is stored locally on your device only and is inaccessible to us.

#### 3.2 Analytics — AppMetrica (Yandex)

The App uses the AppMetrica SDK to collect anonymous usage analytics. AppMetrica may collect:

- Anonymous in-app events (screen opens, user actions)
- Technical device and OS version data
- Crash reports
- Google Advertising ID (GAID)
- Session data and time-in-app

Data is transmitted to Yandex and processed per the AppMetrica Privacy Policy:
https://appmetrica.yandex.com/docs/en/general/privacy-policy

You can opt out of ad personalization in Android settings: **Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization** (or reset your advertising ID).

#### 3.4 Advertising — Yandex Mobile Ads

The App displays banner ads via the Yandex Mobile Ads SDK. For ad delivery, Yandex may collect:

- Google Advertising ID (GAID)
- IP address (for region detection)
- Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
- Technical device information

Collection and processing of this data is governed by Yandex's Privacy Policy:
https://yandex.com/legal/confidential/

### 4. Data Storage and Security

#### 4.1 Local Data

All user data (diary, ratings, bookmarks, watch statuses) is stored in a local SQLite database (Room) on your device. Google Drive backups are governed by standard Android settings and Google's policies.

#### 4.2 Network Requests

All requests to our server use HTTPS with TLS encryption. We do not retain request logs for more than 30 days.

#### 4.3 Retention

Technical request tokens are not stored by us in association with any individual user and are automatically anonymized.
